About Movie

Homeless orphan who survives through small lies and struggles on the streets. His life changes when he meets Savitri, a rich landlord’s daughter who runs away from home. As they travel together, love grows between them, and He eventually helps her reconnect with her family. The movie focuses on love, kindness, sacrifice, and emotional bonding.
