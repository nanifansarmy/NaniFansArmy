About Movie

This is the director’s debut movie. The movie tells the story of Gautham, a simple young man whose life changes after meeting Nithya. Through a series of funny misunderstandings, emotional moments, and unexpected twists, the film explores love, friendship, and destiny in a light-hearted way.
