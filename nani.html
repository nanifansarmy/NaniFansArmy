About Nani

Natural Star Nani is one of the most loved actors in Telugu cinema. Known for his natural performances and inspiring journey, Nani has built a massive following. This page highlights his biography, achievements, career milestones, and impact on Indian cinema.

Critically acclaimed Indian actor and producer with over 17 years of excellence in cinema. Renowned for his natural acting style, unique script choices, and consistent box office success, he is crowned the “Natural Star” for his relatable screen presence, celebrated as the “Overseas Prince” for his global appeal, and hailed as “uNANImous” by fans for the universal admiration he commands.

★ Nani is one of the few Telugu actors to successfully transition from Assistant Director to Leading Star.

★ V became one of the first major Telugu films to premiere directly on OTT, skipping a theatrical release during the pandemic.

★ V and Tuck Jagadish were released back-to-back on OTT platforms during the pandemic era.
