About Movie

A corporate man who goes on a journey of self-discovery after a life-changing experience, exploring themes of purpose, friendship, and spirituality in the Himalayas.Much of the filming took place at the Everest Base Camps in Nepal, becoming the first Indian film to be shot at the location. Director’s debut movie.This film was released along with Janda Pai Kapiraju on the same day, marking a rare occasion where two of Nani’s movies were released simultaneously.
