About Movie

The film follows the fun and emotional journey of Lavanya, who dreams of marrying a man named Mahesh because of her admiration for Mahesh Babu. Unexpected twists lead to hilarious misunderstandings and a heartfelt romance. The movie marked a memorable debut for Nani and was praised for its refreshing storytelling, natural performances, and feel-good entertainment.
