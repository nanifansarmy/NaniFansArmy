About Movie

A young girl suffering from cystic fibrosis who lives with her father. Her wish to know about her absent mother leads to an unexpected encounter that reveals a deep connection to the past, unfolding an emotional tale of love, memory, and destiny. This film was dubbed into Hindi as Hi Papa. Director’s debut movie.
