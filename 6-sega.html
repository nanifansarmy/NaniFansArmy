About Movie

This is the director’s debut movie. The movie is a dark comedy and crime drama that takes place over a single night in Chennai. It follows different characters whose lives unexpectedly connect while searching for money, alcohol, and survival. The film mixes humor, suspense, and urban nightlife elements. Sega marks Nani’s Tamil film debut. The movie was originally released in Tamil as Veppam and later dubbed into Telugu.
