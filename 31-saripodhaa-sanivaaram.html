About Movie

A man using Saturdays to fight injustice and protect the innocent, ultimately clashing with a corrupt and brutal cop who terrorizes a village. This film was dubbed into Hindi as Surya's Saturday and it was released in Japan with the title 土曜日の男 (Saturday Man) with Japanese subtitles.
